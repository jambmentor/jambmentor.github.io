JAMB Mentor

We are JAMB Mentor — an online learning community dedicated to helping students score 300+ in JAMB by understanding core concepts, practicing past questions, and learning the right strategies.

About Us

We are passionate about education, problem-solving, and understanding how JAMB structures its questions. Our teaching approach focuses on interaction, clarity, and real information from JAMB-approved sources.

What We Offer

  • Evening lessons (8:00 PM – 10:00 PM)
  • Structured roadmap to scoring 300+ in JAMB
  • Mathematics and Science lessons
  • Study of recommended texts (e.g. The Lekki Headmaster)
  • JAMB & WAEC syllabus coverage
  • Over 1,000 learning community members

How We Teach

We conduct interactive online classes, usually held in the evening from 8:00 PM to 10:30 PM. This schedule allows students to stay consistent, ask questions freely, and fully engage with lessons.

Our Goals

Our goal is to help students achieve their academic dreams, gain admission into their desired universities, and study their preferred courses. Education empowers understanding, innovation, and real-world problem-solving.

Our Story

We have helped many students achieve their target JAMB scores by providing guidance, mentorship, and consistent support. Our community includes some of the top 300+ JAMB scorers in West Africa.
